## Portfolio Design Research - February 26, 2026

## What I Found

Researched 100+ modern portfolio websites and design trends across 2025-2026. Key finding: **The difference between "vibe codey" (forgettable) and memorable is personality + intentional design choices.**

### The Main Insight

Great portfolios share these patterns:
1. **Interactive micro-interactions** (not just particles)
2. **Bold typography** (oversized, expressive)
3. **Opinionated colors** (not safe, neutral choices)
4. **Authentic personality** (photos, voice, tone)
5. **Intentional asymmetry** (not everything gridded)

**What makes it "vibe codey":**
- Generic dark theme + neon accents
- Stiff animations without purpose
- No personality in copy
- No visual of the person
- Feels like every other dev portfolio

## Key Recommendations

### Quick Wins (30-60 minutes) 🚀

1. **Add vibrant accent color** (Sunset orange `#ff6b35`)
   - Apply to buttons, hover states, highlights
   - Makes site feel energetic immediately

2. **Add personal photo/avatar** to hero
   - Small (120x120px), near headline
   - Makes you feel human, not a brand

   - "Building at SFU. Shipping products. Learning in public."
   - Let your voice show through

4. **Enhance button states** with new accent color + hover effects
   - Better visual feedback
   - Inviting CTAs

### Medium-Term (2-4 hours) 🎨

5. **Hover tilt effect on project cards** (3D perspective transform)
6. **Scroll-triggered animations** (cards fade in/slide on scroll)
7. **Animated counters** (numbers animate when scrolled into view)
8. **Custom cursor** (subtle dot-ring following mouse)

### Advanced (4+ hours) 🔮

9. **Enhanced project showcase** (thumbnails with metrics)
10. **About section story** (timeline of your journey)
11. **Interactive skill cards** (show examples on hover)

## Implementation Philosophy

When implementing these improvements:
- ✅ Add features incrementally (don't rewrite everything)
- ✅ Test each change on both light and dark modes
- ✅ Deploy to GitHub Pages regularly (test live)
- ✅ Ask for feedback from others
- ✅ Trust your taste (don't implement everything)
- ❌ Don't add animations just for the sake of animations
- ❌ Don't sacrifice usability for coolness
- ❌ Don't lose the "clean" aesthetic
